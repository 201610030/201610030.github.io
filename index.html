About The Event

The Computer Science Exposition (CS Expo) is an annual event in FEU - Institute of Technology where the students showcase their diverse research projects in the field of Computer Science. The theme for this year’s CS Expo is “Likha: Filipinos Merging Innovation with Boundless Imagination”, thus focuses on how Filipinos simultaneously adapt to the use of innovations of technology around the world and at the same time develop projects that resolve social conflicts by applying new technological concepts and methods in the field of Computer Science.
