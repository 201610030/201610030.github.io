About The Event

About The Event

The Computer Science Exposition (CS Expo) is an annual event in FEU - Institute of Technology where the students showcase their diverse research projects in the field of Computer Science. The theme for this year’s CS Expo is “Likha: Filipinos Merging Innovation with Boundless Imagination”, thus focuses on how Filipinos simultaneously adapt to the use of innovations of technology around the world and at the same time develop projects that resolve social conflicts by applying new technological concepts and methods in the field of Computer Science.

Where

Innovation Center, FEU Institute of Technology

When

Wednesday
June 26 2019

Projects

Here are the thesis projects which will be presented in the event

BayT



BayT

This application aims to aid users to easily understand the Baybayin script with the use of their mobile devices. By using their device's camera, the users would be able to capture the Baybayin text and the application would transliterate it to its equivalent Roman Alphabet and nearest Tagalog word if possible. Also, there is a section in the application wherein Baybayin is being introduced to the user.


Meat Analizer



Meat Analyzer

This determines the certain type of meat, and whether it is healthy or unhealthy.

SkinCheckr



SkinCheckr

SkinCheckr is a mobile application that can detect and diagnose different skin diseases namely Atopic Dermatitis, Contact Dermatitis, Dyshidrotic Eczema, Intertrigo, Melanoma, Pityriasis Versicolor, Psoriasis, Tinea Corporis, Tinea Pedis, Benign Mole and Healthy Skin. Skincheckr will also show its possible causes and other symptomps, preventive measures, self-care treatments and differential diagnosis.

Cyclometer Training



Cyclometer Training

Cyclometer Training Assistance Application a free user-friendly training application which gives users a way to turn their phone into a mobile advanced cyclocomputer that can track the user's distance travelled, burned calories, speed and rpm. The application also records completed sessions of the user by using a google account and through Google Fit.

Audisync



Audisync

A mobile rhythm game with dynamic game design. Levels are auto generated and designed based on the music features extracted from the user's choice of music.Music can be chosen from their own library of audio files.

Techy Bartender



Techy Bartender

TechyBartender is an Android application that can help you mix cocktails using Augmented Reality. With the help of its liquor bottle detection and recipe suggestion, you will surely unleash the bartender in you.
